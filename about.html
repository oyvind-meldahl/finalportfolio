About Me

Hello there! I'm Øyvind, a down-to-earth individual hailing from the breathtaking landscapes of Norway. I'm 36 years old and just recently wrapped up my education in Frontend Development at Noroff School of Technology. It's an exciting time for me as I embark on a career in the ever-evolving world of web development.

Beyond my love for coding, I find immense joy in being a father to two wonderful young girls and a devoted husband. We live in a cozy home just north of Oslo, where we create precious memories and relish the simple pleasures of life.

When I'm not engrossed in the digital realm, you can often find me in the company of our two furry companions—our beloved cats. They bring an extra dose of comfort and tranquility to our home, offering a delightful respite from the fast-paced nature of technology.

My approach to frontend development is grounded in my practical nature. I strive to create user-friendly interfaces and seamless experiences that make a positive impact. By paying attention to even the smallest details, I aim to contribute to the ever-changing digital landscape while staying true to my humble roots.

Join me on this journey as I combine my newfound skills, family values, and Norwegian spirit to bring meaningful and captivating web experiences to life. I'm excited to create accessible websites that leave a lasting impression. Let's make the web a better place together!
